# Gang Wars Campaign Guide

A campaign system for One Page Rules Grimdark Future Firefight.

## Overview

Three factions battle for control of the underhive. Each faction fields multiple gangs who fight to expand their territory, establish criminal operations, and crush their rivals. The faction with the most total Influence Level across all districts at the end of the campaign wins.

## Factions

- **The Undergrid Syndicate**
- **Static Dominion**
- **Ashfall Covenant**

Each faction has multiple players, each controlling their own gang. Players within a faction should coordinate strategy.

## The Map

The campaign takes place across 9 districts:

- Industrial Core
- Hab Blocks
- Trade Hub
- Cinder Row
- The Dregs
- Manufactorium District
- The Blight
- Freight Spine
- Scrap Town

Districts are connected to each other, and your gang can only move to or attack adjacent districts.

## Influence Level

Each faction tracks an Influence Level (0-4) in each district. Influence Level represents your faction's influence and control. Higher Influence Level gives advantages in battles and affects campaign outcomes.

**You win by having the highest total Influence Level across all districts at the end of the campaign.**

## Campaign Phases

Each campaign round follows this sequence:

1. **Select Operations** - Each player secretly chooses one operation for their gang
2. **Reveal Operations** - The Gamemaster reveals all chosen operations
3. **Resolve Establish Racket** - Players build new rackets
4. **Resolve Turf Wars** - Battles are fought
5. **Resolve Relocate** - Gangs move to new districts
6. **Resolve Subvert Influence** - Sabotage attempts are resolved
7. **Campaign Events** - Random events shake up the campaign
8. **Free Relocate** - All players may move their gang to an adjacent district
9. **Faction Racket** - Each faction may establish one free racket

## Operations

Each phase, you choose one operation for your gang:

| Operation | Effect |
|-----------|--------|
| **Turf War** | Attack an enemy faction in your district or an adjacent one |
| **Relocate** | Move your gang to an adjacent district |
| **Establish Racket** | Build a racket in your current district |
| **Home Turf Advantage** | If attacked this phase, you choose the battlefield |
| **Subvert Influence** | Attempt to reduce enemy Influence Levels nearby |

## Battles

When a Turf War is declared, a battle is fought using Grimdark Future Firefight rules.

### Theatres

Each district has available **Theatres** - battlefield environments that modify the rules. The attacker usually chooses the theatre (unless the defender has certain advantages).

Each theatre favors certain playstyles. For example:
- **Fungal Depths** favors melee gangs
- **The Killing Fields** favors ranged gangs
- **Transit Corridors** favors fast gangs

### Twists

At the start of each battle, roll D6 to determine which **Twist** applies. Twists add special rules based on the theatre - spore clouds that limit shooting, radiation that damages weak models, hidden passages for ambushes, etc.

### Winning Battles

The winner of a battle:
- Increases their faction's Influence Level in that district by 1
- Applies positive Campaign Outcomes (varies by mission)

The loser:
- Decreases their faction's Influence Level by 1
- May suffer negative Campaign Outcomes

## Rackets

Rackets are persistent assets that provide ongoing benefits. Your faction can establish rackets in districts where you have presence.

| Racket | Benefit |
|--------|---------|
| **Stronghold** | Manipulate Influence Level for campaign outcomes |
| **Fortifications** | Minimum Influence Level of 1; protects against reduction |
| **Smuggling Route** | Extra movement options for your gangs |
| **Informant Network** | See enemy plans; choose theatre when defending |
| **Fighting Pit** | Gain temporary fighters after battles |
| **Toll Gate** | Block enemy movement through the district |
| **Safe House** | Ignore negative outcomes once (then destroyed) |

## Campaign Events

At the end of each phase, the Gamemaster may roll for random events:

- **Underhive Upheaval** - General events affecting all factions
- **Hubris of the Mighty** - Penalties for the dominant faction
- **Desperate Gambits** - Bonuses for the trailing faction

These events help keep everyone in the game and add some unpredictability.

## Tips for Players

1. **Coordinate with your faction** - You're a team. Plan your operations together.
2. **Control the theatres** - Pick battlefields that suit your gang's strengths.
3. **Build rackets strategically** - Informant Networks and Fortifications are powerful defensive tools.
4. **Watch the map** - Don't overextend. Protect your territory while expanding.
5. **Adapt your gang** - The campaign rewards flexibility. Consider how your roster handles different theatres.

Good luck, and may the best faction claim the underhive.
